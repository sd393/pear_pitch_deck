appendix · 06 — how we got here

we tried to fix our own dandruff.

Samarjit and I both have seborrheic dermatitis. one late night working on Mandarins, debating dandruff shampoos — we'd both spent $60+ on Amazon-floated products that didn't work. neither of us could tell what was good.

we asked: how did these products even reach us? we built a side project — gifting between brands and creators with no strings, content only if it was real. 10–15 creator conversations later, we launched.

1 week post-launch: 50 creators, 7 brands, $300M total valuation. then we saw the future.

Mandarins solved the wrong problem. Giftly solves the trillion-dollar one.
