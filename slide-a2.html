appendix · 02 — why not Mercor

wrong customer. wrong economics. wrong data.

wrong customer
Mercor sells to AI labs (RLHF for capability training). brand-side affiliate teams are a different buyer with different sales cycle, price point, and metric.
wrong economics
Mercor pays experts hourly. we get eval data as a byproduct of brand-funded creator commissions — zero labor cost to us. capital advantage flips.
wrong data
paid evaluators with no audience produce judgment without behavioral validation. that's structurally what gameable reviews already are.

the real threat is a commerce platform with brand relationships. but the trust layer has to be neutral — same conflict the labs have.
